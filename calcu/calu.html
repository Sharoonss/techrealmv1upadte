Our Mission

At TechRealm, we believe every child deserves access to education, nutritious food, and proper care. We're committed to creating a brighter future for children in need around the world.

Education

We provide educational resources, school supplies, and learning opportunities to help children develop skills for a better future.

Nutrition

We ensure children receive nutritious meals to support their physical development and help them focus on learning.

Care

We provide emotional support, healthcare, and safe environments where children can grow and thrive.

Frequently Asked Questions

How is my donation used?

Your donation directly supports children's education, nutrition, and care. We allocate funds to provide school supplies, nutritious meals, safe housing, and emotional support for children in need.

Is my donation tax-deductible?

Yes, TechRealm is a registered non-profit organization, and all donations are tax-deductible to the extent allowed by law. You will receive a receipt for your donation that can be used for tax purposes.

Can I sponsor a specific child?

Yes, we offer child sponsorship programs where you can support a specific child's education and well-being. Please contact us directly for more information about our sponsorship opportunities.

How can I volunteer or get involved?

We welcome volunteers who want to contribute their time and skills. You can help with fundraising events, administrative tasks, or even volunteer in our programs directly. Contact us to learn about current volunteer opportunities.

What percentage of my donation goes to administrative costs?

We pride ourselves on transparency and efficiency. Over 85% of all donations go directly to our programs supporting children, with less than 15% used for administrative and fundraising costs.
